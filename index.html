Our Story

A kitchen with memory. A table with soul.

In 1956, this corner of Koukaki became a kitchen. Not a restaurant in the modern sense — a place where neighbors came to eat what was cooked that morning. Slow-braised stews. Hand-rolled pies. Moussaka baked in copper trays.

Seven decades later, the copper trays are still here. The recipes haven't changed. The ingredients still come from the same markets. What's different is you — traveling from across the world, looking for the real Athens, and finding it two streets from the Acropolis Museum.

Led by the Little NOE team — crafting healthy, authentic flavors that redefine everyday dining.
